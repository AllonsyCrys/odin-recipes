Prime Meat Curry

Prime Meat Curries can be made by Cooking Raw Prime Meat, Hylian Rice, and Goron Spice together in a Cooking Pot. Link can eat these curries to have some of his Hearts to his Life Gauge. Since high-quality meat is used when making these meals, they have a deeper taste than other curries.

    Ingredients
  • Raw Prime Meat
  • Hylian Rice
  • Goron Spice
      • Steps
  • Open your inventory
  • Grab all the ingredients listed above
  • Throw all the ingredients into the cooking Pot
  • Wait for it to finish cooking
  • Enjoy your Meal!
      • Especial Effects and combinations
  • Hearty Prime Meat Curry: Made by adding hearty ingredients. When they're eaten, all of Link's Hearts will be healed, and he will gain temporary Hearts.
  • Energizing Prime Meat Curry: Made by adding energizing ingredients. These curries will refill part of Link's Stamina if they're eaten
  • Enduring Prime Meat Curry: Made by adding enduring ingredients. Consuming these will completely restore and overfill Link's Stamina.
  • Hasty Prime Meat Curry: Made by adding hasty ingredients. Link will receive a boost to his movement speed by eating these.
  • Spicy Prime Meat Curry: Made by adding spicy ingredients. If these curries are eaten, Link will gain one level of Cold Resistance
  • Electro Prime Meat Curry: Made by adding electric ingredients. Eating them will grant Link one level of Shock Resistance.
  • Mighty Prime Meat Curry: Made by adding mighty ingredients. These curries will increase Link's attack power when they're consumed.
  • Tough Prime Meat Curry: Made by adding tough ingredients. Link's defense will be boosted if he eats these.
  • Sneaky Prime Meat Curry: Made by adding sneaky ingredients. When they're eaten, Link will receive a boost to his Stealth.
  • Chilly Prime Meat Curry: Made by adding chilly ingredients. If eaten, Link will gain one level of Heat Resistance.
