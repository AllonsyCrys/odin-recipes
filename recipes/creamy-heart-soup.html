Creamy heart soup

Creamy Heart Soups are made by Cooking a Hydromelon, a Voltfruit, Fresh Milk, and either a Hearty Radish or Big Hearty Radish together in a Cooking Pot. Soups made with a regular Hearty Radish heal eight Hearts ♥ to Link's Life Gauge and sell for 50 Rupees. Soups made with Big Hearty Radishes heal eleven Hearts ♥ and sell for 70 Rupees. These soups are known to be sweet, and sharing them is believed to bring two people closer together.

    Ingredients
  • Hydromelom
  • Voltfruit
  • Fresh Milk
  • Hearty Radish (or Big Hearty radish, if available)
      • Steps
  • Open your inventory
  • Grab all the ingredients listed above
  • Throw all the ingredients into the cooking Pot
  • Wait for it to finish cooking
  • Enjoy your Meal!
      • Especial Effects and combinations
  • Unlike many other Food, there are no variations of Creamy Heart Soup that grant additional affects.
